About Easy Analytics

Easy Analytics is a user-friendly tool for analyzing CSV and Excel files. It was created by a team of data scientists and developers who wanted to make data analysis accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical skills.

With Easy Analytics, you don't need to know how to code or use complex software. Simply upload your file and choose the analytics you want to view. The tool takes care of the rest, generating easy-to-understand visuals and summaries of your data.
